About GitMindBloom

GitMindBloom is a compassionate mental health management app specifically designed for individuals living with schizophrenia. Our mission is to empower you to take an active role in managing your condition through tracking, insights, and supportive conversations.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide individuals with schizophrenia with accessible, intuitive tools to track their symptoms, manage their medications, and monitor their mental wellbeing—enabling better conversations with their healthcare providers and greater autonomy in their care.

💡 Why GitMindBloom?

Living with schizophrenia can be challenging. Memory issues, difficulty tracking patterns, and managing multiple medications can feel overwhelming. GitMindBloom simplifies this by providing a single, beautiful platform where you can log everything, chat with support, and see your progress clearly.

🤝 For Caregivers & Providers

Share your data with family members or healthcare providers. Generate reports that show your symptoms, medication adherence, mood trends, and chat history. Better data means better care decisions.
